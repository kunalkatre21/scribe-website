Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Eka Scribe

What is Eka Scribe?

Eka Scribe is an AI-powered medical scribe that records doctor-patient conversations and auto-generates summaries, clinical notes, prescriptions, and structured EMR entries with medical-grade accuracy.

Is patient data secure?

Yes, Eka Scribe is designed with the highest standards of security and privacy, adhering to HIPAA guidelines to ensure all patient data is protected. All audio and text data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Works for in-person and teleconsults?

Absolutely. Eka Scribe is built for flexibility and works seamlessly for both in-person consultations and remote teleconsultations, capturing audio from any environment with noise cancellation technology.

How much time can be saved?

Our users report saving over 12 hours per week on average. By automating clinical documentation, Eka Scribe reduces administrative work by up to 80%, freeing you to focus on your patients.

Which languages are supported?

Eka Scribe supports 15+ languages including Hindi, Bengali, Telugu, Tamil, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, Urdu, and more. We're optimized for Indian medical accents and terminology.

How does EMR integration work?

Our Chrome extension allows one-click insertion of AI-generated notes into any web-based EMR system. You can also copy structured text or export JSON formats for offline EMR systems.
